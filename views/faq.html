FAQ

  • How can I submit a game Idea?
    Click contact us and let one of us know.

  • How do I submit my game design article?
    Contact us and email the Webmaster. Please provide a pdf and/or an html file of your article.

  • How often do you update your website?
    That depends on how many projects are currently running. I would say once every two weeks at the least for every project.

  • I wish to start a project with the DarkChaos team. Who do I inquire?
    Please contact Darkmastermindz on the contact uspage.

  • I have emailed and have not gotten a response...
    First of all, that is a statement. NOT a question. There are a whole lot of messages to check out there. If you have not received a reply within 14 days then please send another one. Otherwise, wait patiently.
